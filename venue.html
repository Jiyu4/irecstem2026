Connectivity

Getting There

How to Reach Us

By Air

Fly to Bacolod-Silay International Airport (BCD). The venue is approximately 1 hour from the airport.

By Bus

Take a bus to Escalante City, then a van or jeepney to Old Sagay. Regular public transport is available.

By Sea

Arrive at Escalante Port via roll-on/roll-off ferry from Cebu City. Short drive from the port.

By Car

From Bacolod City, take the North National Road heading north. Follow signs to Old Sagay.
